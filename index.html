Proffessional service with over 10 years experience

I have been creating, maintaining and removing dreadlocks for some time now. I offer a mobile service so all work can be done in the confort of your own home.

Product and Chemical Free Service

To get the best healthy, long lasting, dreadlocks It is best not to use any locking waxs or other products advertised for dreadlocks as many of these will stay in you hair and over time rot, making the inside of your dreads mouldy and unhealthy. This is why I use nothing more than a crochet hook and fine tooth comb when both creating and maintaining any dreadlocks.
