Andrea showing off his locks

I first created Andrea's dreadlocks around 13 years ago, He has regularly come back for maintainance and has wonderful thick and long locks. In the early days Andrea needed to have maintainance atleast once a month along with doing his own, Now they are fully formed and have been for many years, They require much less maintainance visits as long as regular maintainance is done at home.

Jay's Transformation

Jay had previously had Dreadlocks but never kept them over a year as he hated how thick they were made. Jay prefers a thinner dreadlock, With being fairly short it also creates a more bouncible style rather than having long thick ropes. Before the thin dreadlocks I put in Jay had cornrolls, So first we had to take his styling out, Wash the hair and then wait a week without washing before dreading.

Serah hiding her dreadlocks

Serah is demonstrating how easy it is to cover un-maintained roots in this picture. There are many coverings you can buy for when you sleep which are a great idea as they help to keep the locks from mattting together. As always nothing beats good old regular maintainance for best results seperate and palm roll dreadlocks daily.
